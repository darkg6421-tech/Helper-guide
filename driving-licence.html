🚗 Driving Licence – Apply & Renew

Not affiliated with Parivahan. Guidance only.

✅ Steps for New Driving Licence

1. Visit Parivahan Sewa: parivahan.gov.in
2. Select state and apply for Learner's Licence.
3. Fill Form 2, upload docs, book test slot.
4. Pass online test (LL issued).
5. After 30 days, book driving test slot.
6. Pass test, receive permanent DL.

📑 Document Checklist

Age Proof (10th Marksheet/Birth Certificate)
Address Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/Utility Bill)
Passport size photos
Form 1 (Medical Certificate if applicable)
