📌 Aadhaar Update – Step by Step

Last updated: 14 April 2026
Not affiliated with UIDAI. We provide guidance only.

✅ Online Update via myAadhaar

1. Visit official UIDAI portal: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
2. Click 'Update Aadhaar' → 'Update Demographics Data'.
3. Enter Aadhaar number & OTP.
4. Choose fields to update.
5. Upload scanned documents.
6. Pay ₹50 online.
7. Note SRN for tracking.
8. Download e-Aadhaar within 7-10 days.

📑 Document Checklist

Identity Proof (Voter ID/PAN/DL/Passport)
Address Proof (Utility Bill/Bank Passbook)
Date of Birth Proof (10th Marksheet/Birth Certificate)
Scanned copies ready (PDF/JPEG)
