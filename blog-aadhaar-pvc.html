Aadhaar PVC Card: How to Order Online (₹50)

April 10, 2026

Steps to Order

1. Visit myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
2. Login with Aadhaar number and OTP.
3. Click "Order Aadhaar PVC Card".
4. Verify details and pay ₹50.
5. Card delivered via Speed Post within 15 days.
