🆔 PAN Card – Apply & Update

Not affiliated with NSDL/UTI. Guidance only.

✅ Steps for New PAN (Form 49A)

1. Visit NSDL: onlineservices.nsdl.com
2. Select 'Application for New PAN'.
3. Fill Form 49A with details.
4. Upload photo, signature, documents.
5. Pay ₹107 fee (₹101 if communication within India).
6. Note Acknowledgement Number.
7. PAN delivered in 15-20 days.

📑 Document Checklist

Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/Passport/DL)
Address Proof (Utility Bill/Bank Statement)
Date of Birth Proof
Passport size photo (scanned)
Signature (scanned)
