🛂 Passport – Fresh & Renewal

Not affiliated with Passport Seva. Guidance only.

✅ Steps for Fresh Passport

1. Register on Passport Seva: passportindia.gov.in
2. Fill application form online.
3. Pay fee and schedule appointment.
4. Visit PSK with original documents.
5. Police verification.
6. Passport dispatched.

📑 Document Checklist

Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/PAN/DL)
Address Proof (Utility Bill/Bank Passbook)
Date of Birth Proof
Passport size photos (as per specs)
